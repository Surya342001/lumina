## Workspace Types

### Hot Desks
Flexible open workstations for individuals and small teams. Ideal for freelancers, remote workers, and day visitors.
- Price: ₹250–400/hour or ₹2,800–4,500/day
- No commitment required; walk-in or pre-book via Aurbis One app
- Includes: WiFi, ergonomic chair, community café access, locker

### Dedicated Desks
Your own fixed desk in a vibrant shared workspace. Available on monthly basis.
- Price: ₹8,000–15,000/month depending on location
- Includes: dedicated monitor, storage pedestal, mail handling, printing

### Private Offices (2–10 people)
Fully furnished, lockable private offices with customizable branding.
- Price: ₹500–2,500/hour or ₹30,000–80,000/month
- Includes: signage, meeting credits, dedicated internet line

### Team Suites (10–30 people)
Large private spaces for growing teams who need their own environment.
- Price: ₹1,000–3,000/hour
- Includes: dedicated reception, custom layout, branding

### Conference Rooms (8–30 people)
Professional meeting spaces equipped for modern hybrid work.
- Price: ₹1,200–3,000/hour
- Standard inclusions: 4K display, HD video conferencing, whiteboard, high-speed WiFi, refreshments

### Training Rooms (20–50 people)
Fully equipped facilities for corporate training and workshops.
- Price: ₹3,000–8,000/hour
- Includes: projector, workstations, breakout area, recording system

### Event Spaces (50–200 people)
Premium large-scale venues for conferences, launches, and corporate celebrations.
- Price: ₹10,000–30,000/hour (minimum booking: 3–4 hours)
- Includes: professional AV, stage/podium, event staff support

- **Free cancellation**: Up to 24 hours before the booking

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book a space for the same day?**
A: Yes! Conference rooms and meeting rooms can be booked with just 2 hours notice, subject to availability. Event spaces require at least 24 hours advance notice. Check real-time availability on the Aurbis One app or website.

**Q: Is parking available at Aurbis locations?**
A: Yes, limited parking is available at all 5 locations. Whitefield has a dedicated multi-level parking structure. Koramangala and HSR Layout have street-level parking. All locations have EV charging stations. Valet parking is available for Executive Suite and event bookings.

**Q: Can I tour a workspace before booking?**
A: Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a free guided tour at any location. We also offer immersive virtual tours online. Tours are available Monday–Saturday, 10 AM–6 PM.

**Q: Do you offer virtual office services?**
A: Yes! Virtual office plans start at ₹2,000/month and include a prestigious business address, mail handling and forwarding, GST-friendly billing address, and 2 days/month access to meeting rooms.

**Q: What is included in event space pricing?**
A: Base event pricing includes: space setup, AV equipment, WiFi, basic furniture, and security. Catering, décor, additional AV upgrades, and event coordination can be added as extras at quoted rates.

**Q: Are there any long-term commitment requirements?**
A: None for hourly bookings. Day passes and hot desks are commitment-free. Monthly memberships require 30-day cancellation notice. Enterprise contracts are typically 6–24 months with flexible exit clauses.

**Q: Do you serve food and beverages?**
A: Yes! All locations have an in-house café serving breakfast, lunch, and snacks. Our catering team can provide customized menus for meetings and events. Order through the Aurbis One app.

**Q: Are pets allowed in the workspace?**
A: Aurbis is a pet-friendly workspace! Dogs are welcome in open hot desk areas on weekdays. Please inform us in advance. Pets are not permitted in conference rooms or event spaces.

**Q: What security measures are in place?**
A: Comprehensive security at all locations includes: 24/7 CCTV surveillance, biometric and RFID access control, trained security personnel, visitor management system with digital ID verification, and secure lockers for members.

**Q: Can I use workspaces on weekends and public holidays?**
A: Whitefield, HSR Layout, Koramangala, and Indiranagar offer 24/7 access. MG Road is available Monday–Saturday. Some locations may have reduced walk-in hours on public holidays — check the app for real-time updates.

**Q: How do corporate/enterprise accounts work?**
A: Enterprise accounts get dedicated account management, custom pricing, monthly consolidated billing, and priority access across all locations. Contact enterprise@aurbis.com for a tailored proposal.

**Q: Can I reschedule a booking?**
A: Yes, free rescheduling is available up to 4 hours before your booking start time, subject to availability of the new slot.

**Q: What is the Aurbis One app?**
A: Aurbis One is our proprietary smart workspace management app. Use it to: book spaces, unlock doors with your phone, control room temperature and lighting, order food, track your usage, manage invoices, and connect with the Aurbis community.
